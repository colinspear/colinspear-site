I’m interested in the difference between innovation and technology that manipulated the physical world versus that which is primarily digital.

Questions

  • Is there some sort of natural cap on the returns to digital technologies?
  • Will more software, including most AI really impact our lives and the economy in world changing ways? Software may be eating the world, but what are the limits on how much it can change the world?
