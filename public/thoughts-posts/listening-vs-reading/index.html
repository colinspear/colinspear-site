I mostly listen to books these days. With a full time job and two toddlers, there just isn’t a lot of time to sit down. For a while I listened as fast as I could. I was in a real consume as much as possible sort of phase, wanting to be well read. Smart. Lately I’ve slowed down. I abandon lots of books too, looking for the one that really grabs me. Other than the frequent orphans, this is mostly how I used to read
