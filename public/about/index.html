Hello!

My name is Colin. I share my notes, thoughts and a record of my digital wanderings here. I hope you can find something of interest amongst the many things that grab my attention. If you do, I would love to hear from you. You can send me an email at colinspear@gmail.com or find me on Twitter at @colinspear.

By training, I am an economist, a baker, a sculptor and a student of the Spanish language. By profession, I am a data scientist working to provide population data to public health researchers.
